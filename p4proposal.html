A Perfect Day Travel Forum

Hedstrom P4: Final Project

A crowd sourced web-site allowing users to post, read and comment on activity and travel ideas. The "days" could be geographically or topically based (e.g. for children, couples, indoors, outdoors). A perfect day travel will be the place where users search for information by particular keywords or browse the topics for inspiration.

Chowhound is a well developed model for what I have in mind. The forum is constantly updated and has expanded from restaurant suggestions to encompass topics of interest to 'foodies' in general. It's has been highly commercial, but the heart of the site is still the fresh, insightful, really local input of its members.

Obviously, I'm not the first person to think of this. Plenty of sites already exist for travel planning:

insert trip advisor and independent traveler here. - my site would be a place for local insight to benefit a wider audience. Beyond the tourist trade, suggestions for the most wonderful days - wherever you may find yourself. Slow travel if you will.
